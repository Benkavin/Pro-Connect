Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Pangig. Can't find your answer? and we'll help.

For Clients

Pangig is a global contractor marketplace. Here's how it works in 4 simple steps:

  1. Post your job for free — describe what you need, set your location and budget. Takes less than 2 minutes.
  2. Receive quotes — up to 7 qualified contractors in your area will review your job and reach out with their rates.
  3. Compare and choose — review contractor profiles, compare quotes, and select the best professional for your needs.
  4. Hire and pay securely — confirm the job, communicate directly, and pay through Pangig's secure platform.

Yes — posting a job on Pangig is completely free for clients. There are no hidden fees, no subscription costs, and no charges to receive quotes. You only pay the contractor directly for the work completed.

Most clients hear from contractors within a few hours of posting. Response times vary depending on the type of job, your location, and the time of day. For urgent jobs, mention it clearly in your job description to attract faster responses.

All contractors on Pangig go through a registration process and agree to our terms of service. We are actively building out a full verification system that will include license checks, ID verification, and reviews from past clients. We recommend always reviewing a contractor's profile, asking for references, and confirming their credentials before hiring.

Yes. You can close or remove your job posting at any time from your client dashboard. If contractors have already reached out, we recommend notifying them that the position has been filled or cancelled as a courtesy.

For Contractors

Credits are the currency contractors use on Pangig to unlock client leads. Here's how it works:

  • Browse available job listings for free
  • When you find a job you want, spend credits to unlock the client's contact details
  • Credits are deducted only when you unlock a lead — not just for browsing
  • Each job type has a different credit cost depending on the complexity of the service
  • Credits never expire — use them whenever you're ready

Credit packages: Starter (10 credits / $15), Pro (50 credits / $60), Elite (100 credits / $100).

Currently, all contractors can sign up and start accessing leads immediately. Our full contractor verification system — including license uploads, ID verification, and background checks — is coming soon. Verified contractors will receive a badge on their profile, giving clients greater confidence when hiring.

To get started, simply create your contractor account, select your primary service, and purchase credits to begin unlocking leads.

Pangig accepts multiple payment methods depending on your location:

  • Stripe — Credit/debit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay (available globally including USA, Canada, UK, and 40+ countries)
  • Paystack — Local bank transfers, USSD, mobile money, and cards (available in Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, and other African markets)

All payments are processed securely and your financial information is never stored on our servers.

If you purchased credits and have not used them, you may be eligible for a refund within 7 days of purchase. Used credits are non-refundable. To request a refund, contact us at [email protected] with your account email and payment details and we'll review your request promptly.
